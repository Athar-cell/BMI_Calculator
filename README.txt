# 💪 BMI Calculator 🚀

A fun and interactive **BMI Calculator** built with [Streamlit](https://streamlit.io/).  
It calculates your Body Mass Index (BMI), shows your category, and gives you **funny emojis + messages** based on your results. 🎉  

👉 Live Demo: [Click Here to Try It](https://bmicalculator-s3gusxli4dhiva3iztcen2.streamlit.app/)

---

## 📌 Features
- ✅ Supports height in **Centimeters, Meters, and Feet**  
- ✅ Clean and simple **Streamlit UI**  
- ✅ Funny **messages & emojis** for BMI categories 🤣  
- ✅ WHO standard BMI category reference  
- ✅ Fully deployed on **Streamlit Cloud**
